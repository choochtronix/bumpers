# Brrtz Beta Tester Quickstart

Use this short guide when inviting trusted beta friends to try Brrtz.

## Start Here

1. Open `https://brrtz.com`.
2. Open Settings, then sign in with the email Craig invited.
3. Choose a region: Japan, Bay Area, or Los Angeles.
4. Search for synth or pro-audio gear.
5. Try Gear Mode on and off to compare clean gear filtering.

## What To Try

- Save a search with the star in the search bar.
- Open Saved and confirm the search appears.
- Watch one listing with the heart.
- Switch browser or device, sign in, and use Sync now.
- Try both card view and list view.
- Try source pills to narrow results by source.

## Source Notes

- Japan has the deepest beta coverage today.
- Bay Area and Los Angeles currently use Reverb US and eBay US as live search sources.
- Craigslist is parked for beta safety. Brrtz may show Craigslist as a manual search assist rather than scraping Craigslist results.
- eBay is broad and can be noisy. Gear Mode should stay on for normal use.

## Helpful Feedback

When reporting an issue, include:

- Region.
- Search term.
- Source, if obvious.
- Screenshot or listing URL.
- What felt wrong: missing result, noisy result, slow search, broken UI, or confusing wording.

## What Brrtz Is Testing

Brrtz is an invite-only gear radar for finding used synthesizers, drum machines, keyboards, modules, and pro-audio listings across multiple sources. The beta is focused on search quality, sync reliability, mobile usability, and whether the product feels alive enough to become a daily gear radar.
