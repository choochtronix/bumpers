About

Brrtz is a beta gear radar.

Brrtz is a free beta gear radar for finding used synthesizers, drum machines, samplers, modular, effects, and pro audio across regional marketplaces — with particular strength in Japanese sources like Yahoo Auctions and Mercari — always linking buyers back to the original listing.

Multi-source search

Brrtz searches several gear sources from one interface and links users back to the original listing source.

Regional context

The beta supports Japan, Bay Area, Los Angeles, and East Coast source groups, each with region-specific currency and source behavior.

Search quality

Clean Gear filtering, source context, saved searches, watched items, and freshness signals help separate real gear from noise.

What Brrtz is not

Brrtz is not a merchant, reseller, or full listing database. It is a free beta search layer that fetches or prepares source searches and points users back to the source where the listing appears.

Beta direction

The product is being prepared for both human searchers and agentic workflows. The short-term goal is clear crawlable context. The longer-term goal is a safe Brrtz connector that lets users ask ChatGPT, Claude, or another agent to search, save, watch, and monitor gear on their behalf.

Model-aware searches

Brrtz is also testing crawlable model-intent pages that connect classic gear names to live regional searches.
