# Brrtz Ops Roadmap

This roadmap is the operational companion to the product roadmap and beta todo docs. Keep it lightweight and update it when loop priorities change.

## Current Phase

Japan beta with Bay Area and Los Angeles expansion beginning.

## Current Product Goal

Make Brrtz reliable, useful, and low-noise for early gear-search users.

## Phase 1 - Foundation

- Source registry.
- Golden search queries.
- Ingestion health reporting.
- PR QA loop.
- Beta feedback digest.

## Phase 2 - Japan Beta Quality

- Improve source coverage.
- Improve relevance.
- Improve saved searches.
- Improve mobile UX.
- Track failed searches.

## Phase 3 - Bay Area / California Expansion

- Add regional source registry.
- Identify Bay Area / LA music gear sources.
- Add region-aware search behavior.
- Test region filters.

## Phase 4 - Global Portability

- Make source adapters easier to add.
- Normalize location and currency handling.
- Prepare region packs.
- Create repeatable launch checklist for new regions.

## Do Not Build Yet

- Paid subscription.
- Heavy social features.
- Complex marketplace checkout.
- Seller accounts.
- Large redesign.
